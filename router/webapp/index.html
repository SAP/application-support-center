Help

Providing in-app help and frequently asked questions gives your users the ability to try and solve their issues using the knowledge base you create. This reduces the amount of helpdesk tickets and increases user satisfaction.

Product and application owners can quickly and easily create help and faq content directly in the CMS portal. Content is grouped by application and platform, making managing multiple applications simple. Creating content in the CMS portal is done using the rich text editor. This content is automatically available in your application for other users to see.

Release Notes

Release notes keep your users informed about on-going development, revisions and changes to your application. Creating and managing release notes directly in ASC is done using the CMS portal. The release notes can be created without hard coding details into your application, making the notes dynamic and easily modifiable.

ASC is a great place to keep consolidated details of your application release notes for posterity.

Announcements

Keeping your users informed about backend outages, system downtime, new releases and important news can be a challenge from your source code. Delivering a new release just to inform users, is time consuming and logistically a nightmare. The Announcements feature of ASC lets you dynamically create and maintain news keeping your end users in the know.

News articles are displayed only once to each user avoiding repetitive information from being shown. Each active article is also displayed as a "popup" on your application load to ensure your user gets the message.

Content Management Portal

The content management portal lets you maintain the content for all of your applications in a single place. The responsive SAP UI5 portal has rich content editors allowing you create clear and helpful articles for your apps.

The portal also stores important data for your application such as IT Direct Ticket information, backend systems and key people involved to ensure all application data is centralized and consolidated.

Development SDK's/API

Use the iOS feedback service to provide Feedback Shake, Global IT Tracking, ASC, Crashlogs, JamfKeychain and adds new features such as rating and suggest a feature.

Or implement the SDK for your device platforms and provide a consistent look and feel.

Download, and drop the SDK into your development project and configure a couple lines of code to get up and running.
